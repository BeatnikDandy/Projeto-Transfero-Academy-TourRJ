OUR TOURS

Rio Street Art Tour is an organization that promotes the urban art scene of Rio and supports local artists.

Our guided tours take you off the beaten path to visit hidden graffiti hotspots and open air galleries.
Always changing and adapting to include all the new works that appear daily in the city.




SOUTH ZONE
This tour covers the South Zone and Santa Teresa,
visiting two different favelas, the Botanical Garden
and the Lagoa, ending at a gallery bar in Leblon.



NORTH ZONE
Our tour of the North Zone is done in Tijuca
neighborhood. Here we see the true face of Rio,
where the “Carioca” graffiti was born. We end the tour
at a gallery store near the Saens Peña metro.



TOUR WITH ARTIST
On request, we will do a special tour together with
a graffiti artist. The tour will have a unique script,
and we will be able to see the artist in action!



WORKSHOP
Any of the tour options above can be complemented
with a graffiti workshop lead by a prominent local
artist.
